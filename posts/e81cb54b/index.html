线性回归法

训练样本：S={x(i),y(i)}i=1n, x(i)∈Rd, y(i)∈RS = \{x^{(i)}, y^{(i)}\}^n_{i = 1},\ x^{(i)} \in R^d,\ y^{(i)} \in RS={x(i),y(i)}i=1n​, x(i)∈Rd, y(i)∈R

Structural Model: 线性函数 f(x)=βTx+bf(x) = \beta^T x + bf(x)=βTx+b，拥有参数集 (β,b)(\beta, b)(β,b)

损失函数：平方差之和（RSS）

RSS(β)=∑i=1n(y(i)−f(x(i)))2=∑i=1n(y(i)−βTx(i)−b)2\begin{aligned} RSS(\beta) &= \sum_{i=1}^n (y^{(i)} - f(x^{(i)}))^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^n (y^{(i)} - \beta^T x^{(i)} - b)^2 \end{aligned} RSS(β)​=i=1∑n​(y(i)−f(x(i)))2=i=1∑n​(y(i)−βTx(i)−b)2​

损失函数可以改写为：

RSS(β)=∑i=1n(y(i)−f(x(i)))2=∑i=1n(y(i)−b−βTx(i))2=∑i=1n(y(i)−xiβ)2=(y−Xβ)T(y−Xβ)\begin{aligned} RSS(\beta) &= \sum_{i=1}^n (y^{(i)} - f(x^{(i)}))^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^n (y^{(i)} - b - \beta^T x^{(i)})^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^n (y^{(i)} - x_i \beta)^2 \\ &= (y - X\beta)^T(y - X\beta) \end{aligned} RSS(β)​=i=1∑n​(y(i)−f(x(i)))2=i=1∑n​(y(i)−b−βTx(i))2=i=1∑n​(y(i)−xi​β)2=(y−Xβ)T(y−Xβ)​

∂RSS(β)∂β=∂∂β((y−Xβ)T(y−Xβ))=−2XT(y−Xβ)=0\frac{\partial RSS(\beta)}{\partial \beta} = \frac{\partial}{\partial \beta} ((y - X\beta)^T (y-X\beta)) = -2 X^T (y - X\beta) = 0 ∂β∂RSS(β)​=∂β∂​((y−Xβ)T(y−Xβ))=−2XT(y−Xβ)=0

L(β)=∑i=1N{yilog⁡p(xi;β)+(1−yi)log⁡(1−p(xi;β))}=∑i=1N{yiβTxi−log⁡(1+eβTxi}=→−∑i=1Nlog⁡(1+e−yi∗βTxi)\begin{aligned} L(\beta) &= \sum_{i=1}^N \{y_i \log p(x_i; \beta) + (1-y_i) \log(1-p(x_i;\beta))\} \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^N \{y_i \beta^T x_i - \log(1+e^{\beta^T x_i}\} \\ &= \rightarrow -\sum_{i=1}^N \log(1+e^{-y_i^* \beta^T x_i}) \end{aligned} L(β)​=i=1∑N​{yi​logp(xi​;β)+(1−yi​)log(1−p(xi​;β))}=i=1∑N​{yi​βTxi​−log(1+eβTxi​}=→−i=1∑N​log(1+e−yi∗​βTxi​)​

其中，yi∗∈{−1,1},p(xi;β)=exp⁡(βTxi)1+exp⁡(βTxi)y_i^* \in \{-1, 1\}, p(x_i; \beta) = \frac{\exp(\beta^T x_i)}{1 + \exp(\beta^T x_i)}yi∗​∈{−1,1},p(xi​;β)=1+exp(βTxi​)exp(βTxi​)​

该函数曲线与 SVM 损失函数相似，应与SVM有类似的分类特性。

逻辑回归法的输出直接就是概率 p(xi)p(x_i)p(xi​)，很多情况下比 SVM 还好用。
